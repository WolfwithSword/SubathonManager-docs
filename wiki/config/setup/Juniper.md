# Juniper Creates

Merch stores partnered with Juniper Creates are also compatible as an integration in Subathon Manager on a per-product basis.

To start tracking the sales of a product, simply add its product URL and a product name in the **Juniper Creates** tab under **External Services**.

Products are polled every 3 minutes for sales, and for each product with sales in that timeframe produce a subathon event with the amount sold as the quantity. The "User" of the event will be the store name. Sales while the app is offline are not tracked.

![juniperconfig](https://assets.subathonmanager.app/docs/config/setup/juniperpreview.png)

You can set a default value for seconds/points per sale, or override per product.

SubathonManager is not affiliated with Juniper Creates or any merch store.
